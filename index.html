Issyk-Kul
Weekend special tour around Issyk-Kul Lake (1808m above sea ). During this tour, guests will visit the Burana Tower, the Museum of Petroglyphs (built from the II century BC to the VI century AD) and the cultural center Rukh Ordo in Cholpon Ata, the Dungan Mosque and the Russian Orthodox Church in Karakol . In addition, guests will visit the Jeti-Oguz Flower Valley, which is one of the most picturesque places in Kyrgyzstan. This place is famous for its high waterfalls, bizarre red rocks, coniferous forests and alpine meadows.

The actualy price : 6000som

Karakol
Karakol ski resort is a unique in its natural conditions
ski resort. The base is located at an altitude of 2300
meters above sea level, on the slopes of the Tien
Shan, among the majestic coniferous forests. The
mild climate caused by the influence of the ice-free
mountainous lake Issyk-Kul will allow you to get
maximum pleasure from skiing. In winter, the
daytime air temperature rarely falls below -5
degrees, and a large number of sunny days allows
you to ride and sunbathe simultaneously. The ski
season starts in November and lasts until April. All
this time you don't have to worry about snow - it
covers the ground in a dense layer, the thickness of
which varies from 1.5 to 2.5 meters. And you
can visit this tour together with us.

The actualy price : 8200som
